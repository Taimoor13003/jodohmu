04 — 10
The Process

We walk the journey with you — every step

Nine deliberate steps. Each one protects you and brings you closer to a marriage that lasts.

1

We register you

A proper onboarding interview to understand your story, your values, and exactly who you're looking for.

A real person takes time to know you — not an algorithm, not a checklist.
2

We build your profile

You set the filters that matter — deen, character, background, age, lifestyle, family vision. We build & lock your profile around them.

Only people who genuinely fit your criteria are ever brought to you.
3

We send weekly matches

From our verified pool we hand-pick your 2–7 closest matches every week — depending on your package.

You never scroll or chase. The right people are delivered to you.
4

We facilitate the first meeting

A guided ta'aruf in the presence of an imam or a respected figure you trust. You're warmly welcome to bring your family.

A dignified, halal first meeting where both sides open up honestly.
5

Contact by consent only

If both sides wish to continue, contact details are exchanged. If either side declines, nothing is ever shared.

Your privacy and haya are fully protected — no details given without permission.
6

Full background check

We visit the person's home and ask around — parents, community, their imam, and their workplace — to confirm who they truly are.

You step into marriage knowing the truth, not just what a profile claims.
7

Clinical psychology assessment

A professional assessment screens for emotional readiness and hidden red flags — while revealing genuine strengths and compatibility.

You understand the real person beneath the surface before you commit your life.
8

The family gathering

We bring both families together — before or after the assessment — to meet properly and discuss the way forward.

Both families are aligned and involved, so marriage is built on agreement, not pressure.
9

Ready for marriage

Once everything checks out, we confirm the match is sound and support you all the way to the akad.

We stay with you until marriage — we never disappear after the introduction.
