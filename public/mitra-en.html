THE PROBLEM
A growing crisis

The marriage crisis is real — and it's getting worse.

394K+
Divorces in Indonesia in 2024 — equivalent to 1 divorce every 80 seconds (BPS)
70%
Of young Indonesians are unmarried — up from 55.79% a decade ago (BPS 2024)
25%
Decline in total marriages over a decade — from 2.1M to 1.58M (BPS 2014–2023)
Real harm
Broken families, single parents, and social instability are consequences — and they are growing
What we keep seeing

The system is failing the people who need it most.
