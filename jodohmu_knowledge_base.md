## 6. Foreign / International Client Policy

**Established June 7, 2026 — firm rule, no exceptions:**

- **Pearl is NOT available to international clients living in Indonesia**
- **Ruby is the minimum package for all international clients**
- Reason: Indonesian families require full background verification before introductions. Pearl does not include this. Introducing unverified foreign men to Indonesian families is a real risk.
- This rule holds even when the client pushes back or seems like a warm lead.
- Split payment (50/50) can be offered to make Ruby accessible — that is the maximum flexibility.

1. Rp 3,750,000 paid upfront
2. Documents collected: passport, visa, photos, biodata

5. Pool readiness confirmed
6. Rp 3,750,000 second payment collected
7. Profiles begin — 6-month window starts

## 11. Business Model Notes

### Why Matchmaking is Hard
- Trust is everything and slow to build
- Revenue is lumpy — not linear or predictable
- Referral-driven, not cold-sales-driven
- Early clients come from trusted intermediaries, not strangers

### What Makes Jodohmu Work (When It Works)
- Human-guided positioning in a market full of apps
- Islamic structure (ustaz facilitation, wali involvement) creates genuine trust
- Rejection feedback from Pearl drives upsell to Ruby — honest coaching, not fabricated
- Physical presence in Bandung is an asset, not a limitation

### What to Avoid
- Premature spending on office before paying clients demand it
- Multi-city expansion before Bandung is proven
- Multiple hires before revenue justifies it
- Promising profile volume the pool cannot support
- Tying business debt to property before income is proven
